Product & Engineering Models

Interactive 3D models developed within the RASR Lab for assistive robotics, rehabilitation devices, and mechanical systems. Drag to orbit — scroll to zoom. Double-click any card to expand.

About This Work

These models support research in upper-extremity assistive robotics and rehabilitation engineering. Each design is developed with downstream fabrication in mind — tolerances, material selection, and manufacturing method are considered from the first sketch. Models are available in STEP, STL, and native CAD formats on request.
