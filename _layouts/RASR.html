Welcome!

Greetings from the Robotics for Assistive Systems and Rehabilitation (RASaR) Lab! We are a research lab within the College of Arts and Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.

The RASaR lab is decicated to undergraduate research in the field of upper extremity robotics for assistance during Activities of Daily Living (ADL) and rehabilitation post surgery/injury.
